I am now working at the [Institute of Artificial Intelligence, China Telecom (TeleAI)](https://www.teleai.com.cn/) <img src='./images/teleai_logo.png' style="width: 4em;">, working on generative video models, 3D/4D generation, and world models.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science, [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. [Hongkai Xiong](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bB16iN4AAAAJ&hl=zh-TW) and Prof. [Wenrui Dai](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Xg8MhyAAAAAJ&hl=zh-TW). Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from [Northwestern Polytechnical University](https://jsj.nwpu.edu.cn/).

My research interests include diffusion models, AIGC, 3D generation, video generation, self-supervised learning, and domain generalization. I have published 20+ papers, including first-author/co-first-author papers at top-tier conferences and journals such as ECCV, CVPR, ICCV, ICLR and TPAMI. My research has covered a wide range of topics, including image-to-3D generation, controllable video generation, self-supervised representation learning, face anti-spoofing, domain generation/adaptation.

During my Ph.D., I also worked closely with industry research teams at Huawei, and Qualcomm. I have received several academic honors and awards, including the National Scholarship, Special Scholarship.
